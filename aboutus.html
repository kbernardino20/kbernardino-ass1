About Us

Welcome to our corner of the digital world! We're Kenneth and Marietoni Bernardino, a couple hailing from the beautiful province of Batangas in the Philippines. Our journey together has been filled with love, growth, and a shared passion for education and technology.

Kenneth Isaac Garcia Bernardino:

Kenneth Isaac Garcia Bernardino, affectionately known as Ken, hails from the captivating landscapes of Batangas, Philippines. Born on September 20th, 1990, Ken brings a harmonious blend of experience and youthful vigor to every endeavor. With a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology under his belt and currently pursuing a Masters in Information Technology at King's Own Institute, he delves into the intricate world of technology with passion and precision - constantly seeking knowledge and mastery in his field.

Beyond the world of technology, I am an avid learner, always seeking new ways to expand my horizons. My commitment to personal and professional growth is a driving force in both my career and our shared life.

Marietoni Aguirre Bernardino:

Meet Marietoni Aguirre Bernardino, also known as Toni, a captivating individual whose journey unfolds against the backdrop of Batangas, Philippines. Born on November 25, 1992, Toni graces each day with a blend of wisdom and youthful exuberance that creates a presence both comforting and inspiring.

Armed with a Bachelor of Science in Psychology, she navigates the intricacies of the human mind with grace and insight while embracing two significant roles—loving daughter and devoted wife—with unwavering dedication. Her story is a testament to the richness of her cultural heritage, pursuit of knowledge, and profound connections that define her life as tapestry woven with love and understanding.

Our Story

Our relationship, which began on August 14, 2011, has been a source of strength and joy for both of us. Together, we've weathered the storms and celebrated the triumphs, creating a strong foundation for the life we've built together.

Our union was officially sealed on August 28, 2021, marking the beginning of a new chapter as husband and wife. The journey has been marked by shared dreams, mutual support, and a commitment to creating a life filled with love, laughter, and shared accomplishments.

As we continue to grow both individually and as a couple, we invite you to join us on this journey. Whether you share our passion for technology, psychology, or simply enjoy stories of love and growth, we're thrilled to have you as part of our digital community.
