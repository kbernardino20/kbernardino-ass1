From the Heart: A Native Filipino's Journey Across Our Homeland

Mabuhay! Join us on a heartfelt journey through the captivating wonders of our beloved archipelago. From the bustling streets of Manila to the serene shores of Batangas, let's embark on a cultural odyssey that celebrates the rich tapestry of our heritage. This is not just a travelogue; it's a personal narrative—a love letter to the beauty that is the Philippines.

It's more fun in the Philippines

Manila - Nostalgia and New Beginnings:

Our journey begins in the heart of the Philippines, Manila. As a couple deeply rooted in its history, we explore historic sites and savor local delicacies that bring a nostalgic charm to our shared adventures.


Palawan - Nature's Embrace:

Palawan, the jewel of the Philippines, reveals its wonders. From the breathtaking Underground River to the limestone cliffs of El Nido, we immerse ourselves in Palawan's pristine beauty—a paradise that reflects our love.


Boracay - Love in the Sands:

Boracay, witness to the milestones in our love story. Rediscover the magic of its white sands and azure waters with us. Explore hidden corners and experience the transformative power of Boracay's beauty on an intimate journey together.


Bohol - A Cultural Sojourn:

Visiting Bohol, we see the famous Chocolate Hills, encounter the charming tarsiers, and cruise along the Loboc River. This chapter celebrates the island's natural beauty and cultural heritage that connects with our Filipino identity.

Jomalig - The Hidden Gem:

Our journey takes an adventurous turn as we explore the lesser-known gem of Jomalig. From its golden shores to the warm hospitality of its people, Jomalig becomes a testament to the uncharted beauty that our homeland holds.


Baguio - Highlands Serenity:

Baguio is a popular destination with natural attractions such as mountain ranges and bodies of water. It also hosts several historic landmarks and institutions, making it a serene backdrop for exploration.


Batangas - By the Sea:

In Batangas, the tranquil waves caress our hometown's shores. We discover secret beaches, enjoy local dishes, and revel in the peaceful atmosphere of a province that holds the memories of our romance.


Cebu - A Tapestry of Cultures:

Cebu, where we immerse ourselves in the lively culture. From historic sites like Magellan's Cross to bustling markets and the renowned lechon, Cebu is a vibrant celebration of our collective journeys and the diverse fabric of our Filipino heritage.


This blog encapsulates not just the places we visited but the love, laughter, and shared experiences that have shaped our journey. May it inspire you to embark on your adventures, discover the beauty of the Philippines, and celebrate the joy of travel with the ones you hold dear. Salamat sa pag-salo sa aming paglalakbay—Thank you for joining our journey!
